Loggerithm 隱私權政策

最後更新日期:2026 年 7 月 14 日

簡單說:Loggerithm 不會蒐集、儲存、分享或販售您的任何個人資料。沒有帳號、沒有廣告、沒有追蹤。

1. 我們蒐集的資料

Loggerithm(以下稱「本應用程式」)是一款單機益智遊戲。本應用程式:

  • 不蒐集任何個人資料(姓名、電子郵件、電話、位置等)
  • 不需要建立帳號或登入
  • 不包含廣告或第三方分析工具(SDK)
  • 不要求任何特殊裝置權限(相機、麥克風、聯絡人、位置等)

2. 本機儲存

遊戲進度與設定僅暫存於您裝置上的應用程式快取中(用於離線遊玩),不會傳送到任何伺服器。解除安裝本應用程式即會清除這些資料。

3. 網路連線

本應用程式為網頁技術打造,首次啟動時會從以下服務載入遊戲資源:GitHub Pages(遊戲本體)、cdnjs.cloudflare.com(3D 圖形函式庫)、Google Fonts(字型)。這些連線僅用於下載檔案;上述服務供應商可能依其各自的隱私權政策記錄標準的伺服器紀錄(如 IP 位址),本應用程式無法存取也不使用這些紀錄。

4. 兒童隱私

本應用程式適合所有年齡層。由於我們完全不蒐集資料,因此也不會蒐集任何兒童的個人資料。

5. 政策變更

若本政策有所變更,我們會更新本頁面並修改上方的「最後更新日期」。

6. 聯絡我們

若您對本隱私權政策有任何疑問,歡迎來信:pddlingame@gmail.com

Loggerithm Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

In short: Loggerithm does not collect, store, share, or sell any of your personal data. No accounts, no ads, no tracking.

1. Data We Collect

Loggerithm (the "App") is an offline single-player puzzle game. The App:

  • Does not collect any personal data (name, email, phone, location, etc.)
  • Does not require an account or sign-in
  • Contains no advertising or third-party analytics SDKs
  • Requests no special device permissions (camera, microphone, contacts, location, etc.)

2. Local Storage

Game progress and settings are stored only in the app cache on your own device (to enable offline play) and are never sent to any server. Uninstalling the App removes this data.

3. Network Connections

The App is built with web technologies and loads its resources on first launch from: GitHub Pages (the game itself), cdnjs.cloudflare.com (3D graphics library), and Google Fonts (typography). These connections are used solely to download files. The providers above may keep standard server logs (such as IP addresses) under their own privacy policies; the App has no access to and makes no use of such logs.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages. Because we collect no data at all, we do not collect personal data from children.

5. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the "Last updated" date above.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at pddlingame@gmail.com.
